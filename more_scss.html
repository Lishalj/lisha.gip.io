PROGRAMME SPECIFIC OUTCOMES


BCA


Bachelors in Computer Applications (BCA) is  a three-year undergraduate programme. The thrust of BCA is on an application of Computer languages. The course works as a good launch pad for an advanced career in Information Technology. The basic focus of the BCA curriculum is on computer architecture, operating systems, database management systems, software engineering, web technology and computer languages such as C, C++, HTML, Java etc. BCA degree opens lucrative job opportunities in IT organizations like Wipro, Infosys, Oracle, and IBM. BCA graduates are also competing for the relevant jobs in IT department of Government and public sector organizations.


MCA


Masters of Computer Applications is a postgraduate professional programme to help build a shining career in software and application development. Students wishing to opt for MCA are expected to have a robust theoretical and conceptual knowledge of computer languages – usually studied in the BCA programme.

The updated curriculum prepares students for the ever-changing needs and demands of the IT sector. Starting with the basics of programming, computational theory, algorithm design etc., the course steers through rigorous project works and practical exposure in applied computer science.

With an MCA degree one can become  system analyst, web-designer, programmer, manager, to name a few.
